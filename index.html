Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile and web applications developed by Parasmani Sharma, an independent software developer based in India. By using any application developed by me, you agree to the practices described in this policy. I am committed to protecting your personal information and being fully transparent about how data is handled in every app I build.

This single policy covers all apps I develop — for business clients, personal projects, and any future applications.
🗄️
What data is collected
  • ✓ Information you voluntarily enter in the app such as names, contact details, or business records
  • ✓ Images or files you upload within the application for business or personal use

  • ✓ App usage data such as features used — collected only to improve the app experience

How your data is used
  • ✓ To provide and run the core features of the application you are using

  • ✓ Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with any third party for marketing or commercial purposes
  • ✓ Data is never used for advertising or profiling of any kind

Data security

All data in my applications is stored using trusted and secure cloud infrastructure providers such as Supabase or Firebase, which follow industry-standard encryption and security practices. Access to your data is strictly limited to authorized users with valid login credentials only. No unauthorized person can access your information.

I take data security seriously. Every app I build is designed with your privacy in mind from day one.

Third-party services

Some applications may use trusted third-party services to support functionality such as cloud storage, authentication, or payment processing. These services have their own privacy policies and handle data according to their standards. I only use reputable, well-known providers and never share data beyond what is necessary for the app to function.

Each application only requests permissions that are strictly necessary for its features to work. Common permissions and their reasons:

No app I build will ever request permissions it does not need.
